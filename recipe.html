Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

  • Paneer – 200g
  • Tomato puree – 1 cup
  • Butter – 2 tbsp
  • Cream – 2 tbsp
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Heat butter and add tomato puree.
  2. Add spices and cook till oil separates.
  3. Add paneer and cream.
  4. Cook for 5 minutes and serve hot.
