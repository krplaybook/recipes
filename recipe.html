Palak Matar

Ingredients

  • Spinach – 500 g
  • Tomato – 1
  • Onion – 2 medium
  • Peas – 1 cup
  • Kasuri Methi
  • Cumin & Jeera
  • Turmeric, Chilli, Salt
  • Dhaniya, Garam Masala

Method

  1. Boil spinach for 3 minutes.
  2. Cool immediately and grind.
  3. Heat oil in a pan.
  4. Add cumin and jeera.
  5. Add onions and cook till golden.
  6. Add tomato and spices.
  7. Add peas and spinach puree.
  8. Simmer and serve.
