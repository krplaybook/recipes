Palak Matar

Ingredients

  • Spinach ‚Äì 500 g
  • Tomato ‚Äì 1
  • Onion ‚Äì 2 medium
  • Peas ‚Äì 1 cup
  • Kasuri Methi
  • Cumin & Jeera
  • Turmeric, Chilli, Salt
  • Dhaniya, Garam Masala

Method

  1. Boil spinach for 3 mins.
  2. Strain the spinach and put it in cold water
  3. Grind the spinach
  4. Add 4 table spoon oil in a pan
  5. Add Cumin and Jeera seeds
  6. Put fine chopped onions
  7. Roast until golden brown
  8. Add Ginger Garlic paste and roast it for 1 min
  9. Add 1 chopped tomoto and cook for 3 min
  10. Add 1/2 spoon (Haldi + Dhaniya) powder, 1 spoon (red chilli) powder, 1/2 spoon (Jeera Power)
  11. Add 1 cup peas and cooks for 3 mins
  12. Add salt to taste, spinach puree, Kasuri Methi and 1/2 cup water, 1/2 tea spoon garam masala and cook for 3 mins
